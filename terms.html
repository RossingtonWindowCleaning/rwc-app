Simple terms

💳 Payments
Payment is due within 48 hours of your clean. Overdue payments may result in reminders and possible suspension of service.
🚪 Access
Please make sure we can access your property on the day. The first missed visit is free, but after that we may charge 25% to cover the extra travel time needed to come back.
📅 Cancellations
Please give at least 24 hours notice if you need to cancel. Missed cleans can lead to a build-up of dirt, which takes longer to clean next time, so prices may increase slightly. Repeated cancellations may result in being removed from the round.
💷 Pricing
First cleans include a 30% extra charge, as they are a full deep clean to get your windows up to standard before regular maintenance cleans.
⭐ Referrals
Referral rewards are applied once your referred friend has completed their first clean.
🔒 Your data
Your details are kept secure and only used to manage your service. We never share your information.
👍 Service
We always aim to provide a reliable, friendly service. In rare cases, we may need to suspend or stop service if necessary.
